Design Principles




Contrast: Panic Studio
  1. Panic Studio's website effectively draws in the attention of newcomers with their energetic and contrasting color palette.
  2. Their logo, which is quite rounded and unrestricted also offers a stark contrast to their slim and minimalistic font that occupies the rest of the page.


Repitition: Reddit

  • Reddit's website incorporates a lot of repitition in the form of rows upon rows of tiles that display the various content uploaded by users.
  • There is a similar layout for the different subreddits in the "Top Aww Communities" section.


Alignment: Corsair

  1. Corsair's website displays their products in evenly aligned boxes that not only provide visual satisfaction, but also ensure easy viewing of the different item categories.
  2. The two large boxes, which are evenly spaced in between, also provide an area of focus for visitors to see the different features of their products.


Proximity: Open Stax

  • Open Stax's website is very clean and easy to look at.
  • Its minimal layout makes the interface quite intuitive to manuver.
