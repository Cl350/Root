hololive (ホロライブ), is a Virtual Talent agency consisting of Virtual YouTubers owned by Japanese tech entertainment company COVER Corporation.

Tanigo Motoaki [Nickname: Yagoo ] - CEO of Cover Corp and Hololive Production

Notable Quotes: "We provide the most advanced virtual entertainment experience from Japan."
